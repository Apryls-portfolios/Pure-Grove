Who We Serve !

We serve a diverse customer base. Our primary customers work in the confectionery, bakery, culinary, cereal, beverage and health and beauty industries. We provide sales and service throughout the Ghana, United States and Canada through our network of food brokers and distributors. Our Ghanaian distribution points include ports located in the cities of Accra, Kumasi, Takoradi, Cape Coast, Tema, Koforidua, Tamale and Sunyani.

About Us

Established in 1933 (incorporated in 1966), Pure Grove® Inc imports quality coconut-based products from the Ghana. We market and distribute the "Pure Grove" brand of coconut products.

Products

We now offer a wide variety of specialty products including Virgin Coconut Oil, RBD Coconut Oil, Coconut Flour, Creamed Coconut Butter, Coconut Milk, Coconut Cream, and Coconut Water and its derivatives.

Why Work With Us?
